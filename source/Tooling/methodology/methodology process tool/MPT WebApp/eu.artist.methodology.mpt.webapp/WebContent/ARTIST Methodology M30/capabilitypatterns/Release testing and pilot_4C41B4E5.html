Task Descriptor: UMP.A3
The main purpose is to ensure that the new services could be deployed in the environment assuring all the stakehokders are able to operate and use properly the new services.
This activity should be composed by the following actions:
o Determine whether a service and its underlying service assets can be release into the production environment, the first time and subsequent deployments.
o Ensure that business processes, customer, user and service provider interface are capable of using the service properly.
o Ensure that service teams are capable of operating the service
