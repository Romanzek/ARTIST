Activity: MODELUNDER
Produce the necessary (for MODERNIZATION task) “processed” derived models from the “raw” models obtained in MODELDISCO task, thus filtering only the information required for the remainder of the overall process (i.e., Modernization notably). This task relies on the use of various (chains of) model-to-model transformations from the previously obtained low-level initial models to the final derived models to be provided as inputs of the Modernization tasks. These derived models may conform to different metamodels and so represent “views” on the non-cloud system at different levels of abstraction, according to the requirements of the next tasks.
