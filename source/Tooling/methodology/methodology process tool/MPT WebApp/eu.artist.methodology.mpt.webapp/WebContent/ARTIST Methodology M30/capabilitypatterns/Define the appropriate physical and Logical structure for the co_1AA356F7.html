Task Descriptor: MCRP.A2
This activity is the responsible to define the appropriate structure that enables to create a communication mechanism whose objectives are aligned with the business processes.
The idea is to improve customer satisfaction, optimise the view of the organisation from the outside, and use this mechanism as a platform on which to identify new business opportunities.

Steps
Define the Physical Structure

It is important to define the best structure for this mechanism and this selection depends on the service needs (global, local, 24/7, etc.).

Taken into account the SaaS business model the best structure for the helpdesk is the virtual one. The main aim of a virtual service is to utilize the advantages of both centralized and distributed service desks. In a virtual communication mechanism:
• Knowledge is centralised.
• Unnecessary duplication is avoided, with the consequent cost savings.
• A "local service" can be offered without incurring extra costs.
• The quality of service is uniform and consistent.
Define a Logical Structure

The definition of this Logical structure for comunication mechanish is to ensure the following aspects:

- People should be familiar with the protocols for interaction with users: scripts, checklists, etc.

- People should be equipped with software tools they need to log their interactions with users.

- People should know when to escalate incidents to higher levels or contribute to discussions on the compliance with SLAs.

- People should receive training on the company's products and services.
