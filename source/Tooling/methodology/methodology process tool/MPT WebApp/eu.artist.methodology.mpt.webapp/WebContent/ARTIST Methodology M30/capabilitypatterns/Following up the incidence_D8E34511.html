Task Descriptor: IMRP.A9
The objective of this activity is to be able to know in any moment the status of one incidence. For doing this, the actions to do are:
- to check the progress of the actions defined to solve the incidence
- to collect information of the incidence management process
- to communicate if some problems on the resolution to the part affected
