Task Descriptor: UMP.A4
The objective of this activity is to have ready all the aspects that could be modified due to the changes in the new releases. Some of the actions should be:
o Transfer financial assets
o Transfer business organization
o Deploy processes and materials
o Deploy and publish the processes and materials ready for people involved in the business
