Role Descriptor: Modeller
Modeller is the person who produces the software components model representations. A modeller is also responsible for identifying service relationships, establishing message routes between consumers and services, providing efficient service orchestration and choreography methods, creating powerful service transaction and behavioural patterns, offering valuable service packaging solutions.
