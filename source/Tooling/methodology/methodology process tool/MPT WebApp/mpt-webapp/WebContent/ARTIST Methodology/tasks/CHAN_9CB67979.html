Task: CHAN
Determine the channels
Disciplines: Business Model Related Tasks
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Business Analyst
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Sales numbers
  • Sales strategy (including marketing activities, deliverly, etc)
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Sales strategy and communication channels redefined
    • Support processes redefined (incidence management, helpdesk, etc)
      Main Description

      This task shall answer the question of how the company is approaching the customers. The following questions need to be answered:

      -          Which channels do our market segments prefer?

      -          How do we plan to contact them?

      If we plan to offer our SaaS application through several channels, which one is the most profitable?
      Steps
      S1 – Study the company’s strategy for product and services awareness
      S2 – Study how the customers evaluate the value proposition
      S3 – Determine how the SaaS application will be delivered and how new customers can consume it
      S4 – analyse the helpdesk / post-sale services, updating procedures, incidence management, SLA, etc
