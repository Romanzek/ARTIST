      Main Description

      This task concerns the price definition of the SaaS, how it will be billed and the period.

      The determination of the price / fee for the service can be established based on the use of the SaaS, the number of users or more. Thus, a monitoring component fulfilling these requirements needs to be included in the application.

      This task shall also determine the billing procedures that the company will have based on the monitored values and the price structure.
