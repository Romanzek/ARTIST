Main Description
End-user based  Behavioral Equivalence Tool determines the behavioral equivalence between the legacy and migrated applications, by recording series of experiment logs obtained through the testing by end users of both legacy and migrated applications. Experiment logs are abstracted and compared to determine equivalent behaviour.
