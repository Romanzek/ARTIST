      Main Description

      The purpose of this task is to analyse the current software development processes within the company and re-define them.

      The development processes include the methodology followed (i.e. waterfall vs. iterative) and the different phases.

      N.B. While developing cloud-based software, the most common approach is to follow an agile methodology with short iterations. As soon as a feature is developed and tested, it shall be deployed following the updating procedure
